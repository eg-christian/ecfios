1. What is AutoPay? Why should I sign up for AutoPay?

Auto Pay lets you set up a recurring, automatic monthly payment for your mobile bill. When you use your bank account or the Verizon Visa card you will receive a discount of $10 per month for each line on the account. You can also use a regular credit card or debit card, however to get the discount you need to use your bank account or the Verizon Visa card.

3. Why do I only get the discount if I use my bank account or the Verizon Visa card?

The following payment methods do not require a fee for VZ autopay and we pass the savings to our customers.
Accepted payment methods (through a checking or savings account) that provide the autopay Discount:

  • The Verizon Visa® Card
  • Verizon Business Mastercard®
  • Home Banking
  • Automated Clearing House (ACH)
